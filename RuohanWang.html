Path Dependence

Ruohan Wang

Why not deviate from a path when the direction you are taking has been proven wrong? Is it too late to get there by a short distance? Where does one path end and where does a new one begin?

bio

Ruohan Wang is a Chinese illustrator, painter and visual artist with an interest in time, movement, and the transformation of energy. She works on paper and in space, in the digital realm and IRL, where legs as a vital force are non-stop in motion.
